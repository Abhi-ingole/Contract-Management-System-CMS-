About Om Enterprises

Proudly, Om Enterprises is a firm that started in 2013.The company specializes in painting, plumbing, civil structural repair, restoration, waterproofing, and general construction activities.The firm was founded by Laxman A. Gharat.

Om Enterprises has a team of experienced and professional experts in civil engineering services. The company's mission is to provide superior products and services that satisfy customer needs and maintain a high level of ethics, teamwork, and professionalism.The core values of the company include safety, innovation, trust, positive attitude, and excellence.

1.Core Leadership & Expertise

The organization is guided by an expert mentor, Sushank Kirdak, who has completed Civil Engineering.

We specialize in structural, civil repairs, painting, and waterproofing construction projects.Our services are based on mutual trust, transparency, and collaboration.

Mission

To provide superior products and services that satisfy customer needs, while maintaining a high level of ethics, teamwork, and professionalism.

Vision

To provide a safe, productive, fulfilling work environment for employees and legendary service for customers, all while committed to effective strategic growth.

  • Safety: A primary commitment to the health and safety of all personnel.

  • Commitment: Demonstrating a caring attitude and sharing a mutual desire to succeed.

2. Specialized Services Offered

We specialize in a range of civil and painting solutions, including:

  • Structural Repair: Polymer treatments, structural strengthening, injection grouting, and steel reinforcement.
  • Waterproofing: Exterior, terrace, and general waterproofing.
  • Painting: Interior, Exterior, Decorative, and Industrial coatings.
  • Civil Works: Plastering, flooring (Kota, Granite, Chequered tiles), brick work, and plumbing.

3. Quality Control & Work Procedure

The standard work procedure begins with Serve, Inspection, and Estimation, followed by Erection of Scaffolding. Key steps include structural strengthening, high-pressure jet cleaning, crack sealing, and applying the 1st and 2nd coats of paint.

Safety Measures: The work site is secured with safety belts, helmets, goggles, and masks. All laborers are insured under a workman's compensation policy.Safety Engineers visit the site twice a week.
